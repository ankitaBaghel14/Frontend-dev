The Art of Simplicity

By Ankita Baghel | Published on Nov 3, 2025

Simplicity is not about removing features, but about focusing on what truly matters.

Design is a conversation between form and function — both must coexist harmoniously.

"Perfection is achieved not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away."

Every product tells a story. The clearer the story, the better the user experience.

Clarity comes from restraint, not from excess decoration or complexity.
